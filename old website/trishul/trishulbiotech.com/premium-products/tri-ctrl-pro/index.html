Upcrop TriCtrl Pro

Upcrop TriCtrl Pro contains biocontrol agents viz. Trichoderma spp., Pochonia chlamydosporia and Pseudomonas fluorescens. Upcrop TriCtrl Pro acts against various crop diseases (fusarium wilt, bacterial wilt, bacterial soft-rot, root-rot and root-knot diseases) caused by bacteria, fungi and nematodes. Upcrop TriCtrl Pro also contains seaweed extract powder rich in macro and micro nutrients, amino acids, auxins, cytokinins and gibberellins that improves plant health and yield.

Dosage

  • Fertigation: 250 g per acre
  • Add the recommended quantity of Upcrop TriCtrl Pro in the fertilizer tank, mix well and apply through drip irrigation systems.

  • Acts against fungal pathogens
  • Acts against bacterial pathogens
